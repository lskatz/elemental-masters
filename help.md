## 🎮 Quick Start

1. **Type your hero's name** on the title screen.
2. Tap **New Game** and pick the element you want to start with.
3. From the hub, tap **Start Battle**. Use Attack, Special, or Defend until one side runs out of HP.
4. Win battles to level up. Every 5 levels you fight a **boss** — beat them to unlock a new element!


## ⚔ Battle Controls

<div class="help-card" markdown="1">
### ⚔ Attack
A regular hit using your active element. Always available, and it builds up energy for your special move.
</div>

<div class="help-card" markdown="1">
### ✨ Special
A powerful elemental attack that does double damage. Costs energy — you'll see the purple bar fill up as you attack and defend. The special's name and power level up as your hero gets stronger.
</div>

<div class="help-card" markdown="1">
### 🛡 Defend
Brace yourself! The next hit you take is cut in half, and you still gain a little energy. Useful when your HP is low or you need one more turn before unleashing a special.
</div>


## 🔮 The Element Chart

Each element has a **weakness**. Attack with the element that beats your enemy and your hits do extra damage — we call this *super effective*.

**💡 Tip for new players:** Bosses always tell you which element they use, and the game hints at what beats them. Read the boss intro!
{:.help-tip}

| If the enemy is… | Beat it with… |
|------------------|---------------|
| 🔥 Fire          | 💧 Water      |
| 💧 Water         | 🔥 Fire       |
| 💨 Wind          | 🪨 Earth      |
| 🪨 Earth         | 💨 Wind       |
| ⚡ Lightning      | 🌫️ Dust       |
| 🌫️ Dust          | ⚡ Lightning   |
| ❄️ Ice           | 🌋 Lava       |
| 🌋 Lava          | ❄️ Ice        |
{:.help-chart}

Each pair beats the other — so if water beats fire, fire also beats water. Remember the four pairs and you remember the whole chart!
{:.muted}


## 👾 Enemy Types

<div class="help-card" markdown="1">
### Elemental mobs
Most regular enemies have an element. If you have the matching element, your attacks do **1.5×** damage.
</div>

<div class="help-card" markdown="1">
### Neutral mobs
About 1 in 4 regular enemies has no element — like Cave Bats, Goblins, or Skeletons. They're a bit tougher to make up for it. No element beats them — you just have to hit hard!
</div>

<div class="help-card help-card--boss" markdown="1">
### ⚠ Bosses
Every 5th level is a boss. Bosses are always elemental, and their weakness gets a **2×** damage bonus. Try to fight them with the element that beats them!
</div>


## 📈 Leveling Up

- Win a battle → gain a level. Your HP refills automatically.
- At levels **5, 10, and 15**, you fight a boss. Beating them unlocks a new element you can use.
- You can hold up to **4 elements**. Switch between owned elements at the hub.
- Every 5 levels, your special attack upgrades. Fire goes Flame → Fireball → Inferno → Meteor!


## 💾 Saving Your Game

The game saves automatically to your browser every time something important happens — leveling up, switching elements, beating a boss. Closing the tab is fine; come back any time and tap **Continue**.

<div class="help-card" markdown="1">
### Saving to a file
Tap **💾 Save to File** on the hub to download a backup. Useful for:

- Moving to a different device or browser.
- Keeping a backup in case your browser clears its data.
- Saving your favorite hero before trying something risky.

To load a save, tap **Load Save File** on the title screen and pick the JSON file.
</div>

⚠ If you leave the page after making progress that hasn't been exported to a file, the browser will ask if you're sure. Your auto-save is still safe — the warning is just a reminder that file backups are a good idea.
{:.help-tip}


## 🧠 Strategy Tips

- **Save your special for tough enemies.** Bosses and neutral mobs hit hard. Specials do double damage and end fights faster.
- **Defend before a boss's big hit** if your HP is getting low. Half damage is much better than a knockout.
- **Switch elements at the hub** if you know a boss is coming up. Fighting a fire boss with water is way easier than with earth.
- **Don't worry about losing!** You go right back to the same battle with full HP. Try a different strategy.
